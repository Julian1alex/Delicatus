Our Story

The story of Delicatus is short, and sweet. The story starts off with two chefs; Mitchie, and Alexi. These two young and aspiring chefs met in Italy during a world renowned competition. The fire and competitiveness in these chefs was on a whole different level. At one point during the competition these chefs reached the final four. The last semi-finals involved being paired with another chef; you guessed it. That very pairing was Alexi and Mitchie! These chefs combined their techniques, and secrets; the result was unexplainable. Crowds started to weep with joy as just the very smell was enough to stir the people. The judges themselves were in awe; one particular judge who was an old, and I mean very old man uttered just one word with tears of joy "Delicatus!".

Fast forward 5 years, in the heart of Murica'! The best fine dining experience in the world was Delicatus! Mitchie and Alexi managed to apply their phenomenal skills and re-define what food was. We know that by the end of your experience with us; you will utter one word with tears of joy and that is "Delicatus".

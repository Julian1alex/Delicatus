Our Story

It all started in a quiet stormy little part of italy. One day a man said "hey, I got an idea!" "We can make a wonderful falafal with our ingredients for breakfast burritos!" So he was off to it, he traveled all over the world to source the finest of ingredients to use in a grand restaurant.

Over the years, through hard work and several world wars, the restaurant came to be known as the place to be by the many who visited the land.

Many people from all walks of life would travel hundreds of miles just to experience the food that was being prepared by the master chef. Many of those traveled on horseback.

We have been lucky enough to be visited by several people of fame. some of those include the likes of...

"I say, I do delcare this restaurant to be on of the finest establishments in the area."

"It's a mighty fine place to lay low while waitin' for johnny law to cool off and move on. I have never before been met with such hospitable folk."
